SIGNS AND SYMPTOMS
  • Fever.
  • Weakness and fatigue.
  • Aches and pains, such as severe headache, muscle and joint pain.
  • Sote throat.
  • Loss of appetite.
  • Gastrointetinal symptoms including abdominal pain, diarrhea and vomiting.
  • Unexplained hemorrhaging, bleeding or bruising in all oppenings on the body.
  • Red eyes, skin rash, hiccups and this comes on the late stage of the virus.
PREVENTIONAL MEASURES OF EVD
  • Avoid contact with blood and body fluids such as urine, feaces, saliva,
    sweat, vomit, brest milk, amniotic fluid, semen and vaginal fluids of poeple who are sick.
  • Avoid contact with semen from a man who has recovered from EVD until testing
    shows that the virus is gone from the semen.
  • Avoid contact with items that may have come in contact with an infected person's
    blood or body fluids such as clothes, beddings, needles and medical equpments.
  • Avoid contact with bats, forest antelopes and non human primates such as monkeys
    and chimpanzees blood, fluids or raw meat prepared from these or unkown animals.
  • Follow the CDC guidelines for cleaning, disinfecting and managing waste.
  • Isolate the patient in a private room with a private bathroom or covered, bedside
    commode and close the door
  • Engaging local leaders in prevention programs and messaging along with careful policy
    implementations at the national and global level.
  • Avoid funerals or burial practices that involves touching the body of someone who died
    from EVD or suspect of EVD.
HOW EVD TRANSMITS
  • Blood or body fluids such as urine,saliva,sweat,feaces,vomits,breast milk
    ,amniotic fluid and semen of a person who is sick with or has died from Ebola virus disease.
  • Infected fruit bats or nonhuman primates such as apes and monkeys.
  • Objects such as clothes,bleeding,needles and medical equipments contaminated
    with body fluids from the person who is sick with or has died from EVD
  • Semen from man who recovered from EVD the virus can remine in certain body fluids
    even if they nolonger have syptoms of severe illness
